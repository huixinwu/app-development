Bees

Bees are small, flying insects that are very important to our world. They have black and yellow stripes and can often be seen buzzing around flowers.

Bees are special because they help plants grow by spreading pollen from one flower to another. This is called pollination. Without bees, many of the fruits, vegetables, and flowers we love wouldn't be able to grow!

Dogs

Dogs are furry animals that are often kept as pets. They can be big or small and come in many different colors and shapes. Dogs are known for being friendly and loving. They like to play, go for walks, and some even like to do tricks! They are great friends to have and can make you very happy!
